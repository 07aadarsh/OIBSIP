Tribute to Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer who currently captains the India national team. He is considered one of the best batsmen in the world and has set numerous records in international cricket.

  • Born: November 5, 1988 (age 36 years), Delhi, India
  • Height: 1.75 m
  • Spouse: Anushka Sharma (m. 2017)
  • Teams: India national cricket team, Royal Challengers Bangalore, Delhi cricket team

Achievements

  • Fastest batsman to reach 10,000 runs in ODI cricket
  • Most centuries in international cricket as captain
  • ICC Cricketers of the Year (2017, 2018)
